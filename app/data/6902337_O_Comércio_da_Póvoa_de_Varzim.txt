6902337	O Comércio da Póvoa de Varzim	O Comércio da Póvoa de Varzim, founded in 1903, is one of the three main local newspapers of Póvoa de Varzim, Portugal. Unlike its rivals, Póvoa Semanário and A Voz da Póvoa, the paper is devoted to national and local news alike. ==References== Category:1903 establishments in Portugal Category:Mass media in Póvoa de Varzim Category:Newspapers published in Portugal Category:Portuguese- language newspapers Category:Newspapers established in 1903
