45312342	O with breve (Cyrillic)	O with breve (О̆ о̆; italics: О̆ о̆) is a letter of the Cyrillic script. In all its forms it is a homoglyph of the Latin letter O with breve (Ŏ ŏ Ŏ ŏ).Characters may not combine well on some computers. This will cause О̆ & Ŏ to look different. O with breve is used in the Itelmen and Khanty languages.
