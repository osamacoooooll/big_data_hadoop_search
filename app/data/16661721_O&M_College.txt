16661721	O&M College	Organization & Method College or (O&M; College) is a university in Santo Domingo in the Dominican Republic. It was founded in 1966 by José Rafael Abinader. It has campus in other cities as well, Santiago de los Caballeros, La Romana, San José de Ocoa, San Felipe de Puerto Plata, Moca, Santo Domingo Este. ==External links== * Official Page Category:Universities in the Dominican Republic Category:Education in Santo Domingo
