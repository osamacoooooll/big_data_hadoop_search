22785496	O Song-suk	O Song-suk (born 2 September 1977) is a North Korean long-distance runner who competes in the marathon. Her personal best time is 2:31:14 hours, achieved in April 2002 in Pyongyang. She finished 23rd in the marathon at the 2005 World Championships. She also won the 2004 Pyongyang Marathon.
