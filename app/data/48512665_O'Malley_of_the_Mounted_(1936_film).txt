48512665	O'Malley of the Mounted (1936 film)	O'Malley of the Mounted is a 1936 American Western film directed by David Howard and written by Frank Howard Clark and Daniel Jarrett. The film stars George O'Brien, Irene Ware, Stanley Fields, James Bush, Victor Potel and Reginald Barlow.
