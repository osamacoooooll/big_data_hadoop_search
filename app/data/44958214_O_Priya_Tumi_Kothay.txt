44958214	O Priya Tumi Kothay	O Priya Tumi Kothay (Bengali: ও প্রিয়া তুমি কোথায়) is a Bengali song by singer Asif Akbar. The album O Priya Tumi Kothay was released on 2001.The meaning of the song name in English is O Darling where are you? The song gained most popularity with home and also abroad.
