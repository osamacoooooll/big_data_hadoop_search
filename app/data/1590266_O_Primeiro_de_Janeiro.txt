1590266	O Primeiro de Janeiro	O Primeiro de Janeiro (lit. the first of January) was a Portuguese daily newspaper published in Porto, Portugal. ==History and profile== O Primeiro de Janeiro was based in Porto where it was founded in 1868. Its title is a reference to the reform movement initiated on 1 January.
