37608108	O'Garney Park	O'Garney Park () is a GAA stadium in Sixmilebridge, County Clare, Ireland. It is the home of Sixmilebridge GAA club and is one of the main grounds of Clare GAA's Gaelic football and hurling teams. It can hold about 7,000 spectators, including an 800-seater stand.
