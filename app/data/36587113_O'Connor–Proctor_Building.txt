36587113	O'Connor–Proctor Building	The O'Connor–Proctor Building on N. Main in Victoria, Texas was built in 1895 at cost of $7,721. It was designed by architect Paul Helwig and its exterior is "St. Louis red brick trimmed with Kerrville white limestone". It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1986.
