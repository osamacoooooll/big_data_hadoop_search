68898623	O Ses Türkiye (season 10)	The tenth season of the singing competition O Ses Türkiye (English: The Voice Turkey) premiered on 2 October 2021, at TV8 and broadcasts at 20:00 every Saturday. Murat Boz and Beyazıt Öztürk are the returning coaches from season nine, being joined by former O Ses Çocuklar (kids' version) coach Oğuzhan Koç, and Ebru Gündeş who last coached in season five.
