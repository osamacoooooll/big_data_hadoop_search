48545712	O'Brien Is Tryin' to Learn to Talk Hawaiian	"O'Brien is Tryin' to Learn to Talk Hawaiian" is a World War I era song written by Al Dubin and Rennie Cormack in 1916. ==Reception== "O'Brien is Tryin' to Learn to Talk Hawaiian" peaked at number two on the US song charts in January 1917. ==Lyrics== ==References== Category:1916 songs Category:Songs of World War I Category:Songs with lyrics by Al Dubin Category:Victor Records singles
