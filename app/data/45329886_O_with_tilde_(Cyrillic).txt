45329886	O with tilde (Cyrillic)	O with tilde (О̃ о̃; italics: О̃ о̃) is a letter of the Cyrillic script. In all its forms it looks exactly like the Latin letter O with tilde (Õ õ Õ õ). O with tilde is used in the Khinalug language where it represents a nasalized close-mid back rounded vowel . ==See also== *Õ õ : Latin letter Õ - an Estonian and Silesian letter *Cyrillic characters in Unicode ==References== Category:Cyrillic letters with diacritics Category:Letters with tilde
