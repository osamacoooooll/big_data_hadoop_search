33357539	O'a Caldera	The O'a Caldera, also known as Shala, is a volcanic caldera in Ethiopia. It has two lakes: Lake Shala and a small maar called Lake Chitu. Sub-features include Mount Fike (a pyroclastic cone) and Mount Billa (a cinder cone). ==References== Category:Volcanoes of Ethiopia Category:Calderas of Ethiopia Category:Rift volcanoes Category:VEI-7 volcanoes
