6134526	O Mundial	O Mundial is a newspaper in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. It was founded in 1972 and published until 1976. In 1991, it resumed publication as a monthly. The primary readership for O Mundial is the Portuguese community of Winnipeg.
