44923108	O Espetáculo do Circo dos Horrores	O Espetáculo Dos Circo Dos Horrores (English: The Horrors Of Circus Spectacle) is the seventh album of Brazilian hip hop group Facção Central. == Success == O Espetáculo Dos Circo Dos Horrores has been considered one of the most classic albums of Brazilian hip hop because of its strong lyrics and heavy beats.
