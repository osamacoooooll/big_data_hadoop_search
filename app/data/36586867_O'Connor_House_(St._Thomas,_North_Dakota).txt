36586867	O'Connor House (St. Thomas, North Dakota)	The O'Connor House near St. Thomas, North Dakota is a Queen Anne style house that was built in 1898. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1990. It was home of Archie M. O'Connor and his family. O'Connor was one of the earliest settlers of the area, arriving in 1881.
