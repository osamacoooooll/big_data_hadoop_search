36569502	O Sport, You Are Peace!	O Sport, You Are Peace! ( transliterated as O sport, ty - mir!) was a 1981 documentary film directed by Yuri Ozerov. It showed the opening and closing ceremonies, and sporting events of the 1980 Summer Olympics held in Moscow. The director was awarded the State Prize of the USSR in 1982. The film was selected as the Soviet entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 54th Academy Awards, but did not reach the finals.Margaret Herrick Library, Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences ==See also== *List of submissions to the 54th Academy Awards for Best Foreign Language Film *List of Soviet submissions for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film ==References== ==External links== * * Category:1981 films Category:1980 Summer Olympics Category:Documentary films about the Olympics Category:Mosfilm films Category:1980s Russian-language films Category:Soviet documentary films Category:Soviet sports films Category:Films about the 1980 Summer Olympics
