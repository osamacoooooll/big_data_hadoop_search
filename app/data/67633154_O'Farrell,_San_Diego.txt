The community borders the neighborhoods of Encanto to the north, Skyline and North Bay Terrace to the east, Alta Vista to the south, and Valencia Park to the west. == Government == O'Farrell is located within the 4th City Council District of San Diego. The district is currently represented by Monica Montgomery Steppe, who assumed office in 2018.
