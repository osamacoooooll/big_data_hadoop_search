50883286	O Outro	O Outro is a Brazilian telenovela produced and broadcast by TV Globo. It premiered on 23 March 1987 and ended on 10 October 1987, with a total of 173 episodes. It's the thirty seventh "novela das oito" to be aired on the timeslot.
