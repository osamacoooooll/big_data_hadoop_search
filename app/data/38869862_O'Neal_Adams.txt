38869862	O'Neal Adams	Howard O'Neal Adams (January 21, 1919 – October 27, 1998), also known as Neal Adams,Neal Adams NFL & AFL Football Statistics – Pro-Football-Reference.com was an American professional football and basketball player. Born in El Paso, Arkansas, Adams played both sports for the Arkansas Razorbacks. Professionally, Adams played as an end in the National Football League for the New York Giants (1942–1945) and in the All-America Football Conference for the Brooklyn Dodgers (1946–1947).
