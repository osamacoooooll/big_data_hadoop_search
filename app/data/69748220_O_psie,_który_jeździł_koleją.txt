69748220	O psie, który jeździł koleją	O psie, który jeździł koleją (from Polish: About the dog that traveled by train) is 1967 Polish-language children's book by Roman Pisarski. The book was based on the life of Lampo, a dog that became famous for his rail journeys across Italy in the 1950s and 1960s. It is a school reading in the third-grade classes of primary schools in Poland.
