34049319	O Dono do Mar	O Dono do Mar is a Brazilian film of 2006. == Synopsis == The owner of the Sea delves into the mythical world of the fishermen of Maranhão, through the life of Antão Cristório (Jackson Costa). Created in the hardness of the labors of the sea, this man of strong character loses a child Jerumenho still very young, murdered by the knife of a cuckolded husband, a party of Bumba-meu-boi. The unsurpassed loss of beloved son, prepared by his father from childhood, to succeed him at work in the field of fisheries and the secrets of the great sea of Antão Cristório takes forever the prospect of the future.
