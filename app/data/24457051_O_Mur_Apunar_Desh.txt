24457051	O Mur Apunar Desh	O Mur Apunar Dekh () is the state and traditional song of Assam, India. It was written by Lakshminath Bezbarua and the tune was made by Kamala Prasad Agarwala. It was first published in 1909 in an Assamese magazine named Bahi ("flute"). It was officially adopted as the Assam's state song in 1927 at asom chatrô sônmilôn ("Assam Student Conference") held in Tezpur.Encyclopedia of Indian Literature Volume 1, 1989, pp. 416-418.
