50923964	O Santo Mestiço	O Santo Mestiço is a Brazilian telenovela produced and broadcast by TV Globo. It premiered on 12 February 1966 and ended on 7 June 1966, with a total of 80 episodes. It's the fourth "novela das sete" to be aired at the timeslot.
