30512780	O'Ryan (singer)	O'Ryan Omri Browner (born February 12, 1987) is an American R&B; singer. He is the younger brother of R&B; singer Omarion. He was set to release a self- titled album in 2004 which aimed at the teen pop audience. ==Background== He dated American singer Jhene Aiko from 2005 to 2008.
