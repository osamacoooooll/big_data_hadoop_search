24619781	O Parrulo FS	O Parrulo Fútbol Sala is a futsal club based in Ferrol, city of Spain. Its pavilion is Pavillón A Malata with capacity of 6,000 seaters. The main sponsor is Estrella Galicia. The team played 5 seasons in División de Honor.
