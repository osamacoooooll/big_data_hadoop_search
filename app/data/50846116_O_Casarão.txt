50846116	O Casarão	O Casarão is a Brazilian telenovela produced and broadcast by TV Globo. It premiered on 7 June 1976 and ended on 10 December 1976, with a total of 168 episodes. It's the seventeenth "novela das oito" to be aired on the timeslot.
