21239731	O'Neil Wilson	O'Neil Wilson (born January 17, 1978 in Scarborough, Ontario) is a Canadian football wide receiver who is currently a free agent. He has previously played for the BC Lions of the Canadian Football League. He was drafted in the 3rd round of the 2004 CFL Draft by the Montreal Alouettes. He played college football at UConn. Wilson has also played for the Winnipeg Blue Bombers and Hamilton Tiger-Cats.
