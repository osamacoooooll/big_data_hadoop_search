It covers an area of 140.1 sq.km, and the overall population of this comarca was 83,305 at the 2011 Census;Census at 1 November 2011: Instituto Nacional de Estadística, Madrid. the latest official estimate (as at the start of 2018) was 82,445.Estimate at 1 January 2018: Instituto Nacional de Estadística, Madrid. It is bounded on the north by the estuary of Pontevedra, to the east with the comarca of Pontevedra, to the south with the Vigo estuary and to the west by the Atlantic Ocean. The current comarca of the Morrazo corresponds to only part of the traditional region. Apart from the municipalities that compose it, it is traditionally part of Vilaboa as well (minus its parish of Bértola and that of Figueirido and neighborhood of Paredes in the parish of Vilaboa). The Pontevedra parishes of Louizán also forms part of the historical O Morrazo and most of the Salcedo parish.
