8181352	O'Neil Richards	O'Neil Richards (born 7 December 1976 in Jamaica) is a Jamaican cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and right-arm fast-medium bowler. He played five first-class and three List A matches for Jamaica between 1996 and 1999.
