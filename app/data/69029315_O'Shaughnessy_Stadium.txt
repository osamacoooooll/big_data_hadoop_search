69029315	O'Shaughnessy Stadium	O'Shaughnessy Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in St. Paul, Minnesota. It is the home of the St. Thomas Tommies football and track and field teams, in addition to various intramural sports. The stadium opened in 1948 and is named for Ignatius O'Shaughnessy, an alumnus of St. Thomas and a donor to the school. The stadium was most recently renovated in 2017 with the addition of a new field turf surface and track surface.
