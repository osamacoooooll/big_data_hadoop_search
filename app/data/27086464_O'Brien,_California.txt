27086464	O'Brien, California	O'Brien is an unincorporated community in Shasta County, California, United States. O'Brien is located along Interstate 5 north of Redding. O'Brien has a post office with ZIP code 96070, which was established in 1945.ZIP Code Lookup The community is named after Con O'Brien, who had a resort in the community. ==References== Category:Unincorporated communities in California Category:Unincorporated communities in Shasta County, California
