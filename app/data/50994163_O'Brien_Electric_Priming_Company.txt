50994163	O'Brien Electric Priming Company	O'Brien Electric Priming Company is a historic industrial building located at South Bend, St. Joseph County, Indiana. It consists of four sections built between 1882 and 1917. The building housed the O'Brien Corporation, a paint manufacturer.
