45625974	O Magrane	Olympique de Magrane (), known as O Magrane or simply OM for short, is an Algerian football club based in Magrane, El Oued Province.The club was founded in 1985 and its colours are red and White. Their home stadium, Teksebt Stadium, has a capacity of 9,000 spectators. The club is currently playing in the Algerian Ligue 2. ==History== In May 2023, O Magrane was promoted to the Algerian Ligue 2. ==References== ==External links== * Category:Football clubs in Algeria Category:El Oued Province Category:Sports clubs and teams in Algeria
