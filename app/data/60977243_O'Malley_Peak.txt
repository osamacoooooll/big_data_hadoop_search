60977243	O'Malley Peak	O'Malley Peak is a mountain summit located in the Chugach Mountains, in Anchorage Municipality in the U.S. state of Alaska. O'Malley Peak is situated in Chugach State Park, southeast of downtown Anchorage, west of Mount Williwaw, and northwest of The Ramp, which is its nearest higher peak. Access is via the Powerline Trail with several scramble routes to the summit. ==Dr. James O'Malley== "Doc" O'Malley moved to Anchorage in 1946 and practiced medicine there until his death in 1974. He was one of the first doctors in town, respected by his peers and beloved by patients. There is also a school and road named after him in Anchorage.Anchorage Daily Times - obituary of Dr. O'Malley - August 12, 1974 ==Climate== Based on the Köppen climate classification, O'Malley Peak is located in a subarctic climate zone with long, cold, snowy winters, and mild summers. Temperatures can drop below −20 °C with wind chill factors below −30 °C.
