62510014	O'Connor Cup	The O'Connor Cup may refer to: * HEC O'Connor Cup/Michael O'Connor Cup, a Ladies' Gaelic football intervarsity competition. * O'Connor Cup, awarded to the winners of the Munster Senior Club Football Championship. * Michael O'Connor Cup, awarded to the winners of the Kerry Club Football Championship. * O'Connor Cup, a trophy awarded at the Head of the River regatta. * Liam O'Connor Cup, an inter county under-20 Gaelic football competition. * Tom O'Connor Cup, featuring Ladies' Gaelic football teams affiliated with London GAA * James O'Connor Cup, featuring teams affiliated with Leinster Rugby.
