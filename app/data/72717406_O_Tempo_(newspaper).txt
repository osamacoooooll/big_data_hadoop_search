72717406	O Tempo (newspaper)	O Tempo is a Brazilian newspaper based in Contagem, in the Greater Belo Horizonte, state of Minas Gerais. The newspaper was created in 1996 by the Italian businessman and politician who became a naturalized Brazilian Vittorio Medioli and is owned by Grupo SADA, through its publisher, Sempre Editora, which also controls the tabloid Super Notícia and a radio station, Rádio Super.
