33172624	O Bem-Amado	O Bem-Amado is a Brazilian telenovela that first aired on Rede Globo in 1973. It is based on a play by Dias Gomes called Odorico, o Bem-Amado ou Os Mistérios do Amor e da Morte, written in 1962. It was the first Brazilian color telenovela. It was shot in Rio de Janeiro. A notable feature of this telenovela was its music, composed by Vinícius de Moraes and Toquinho. ==Cast== * Paulo Gracindo - Odorico Paraguaçu * Lima Duarte - Zeca Diabo (José Tranquilino da Conceição) * Emiliano Queiroz -
