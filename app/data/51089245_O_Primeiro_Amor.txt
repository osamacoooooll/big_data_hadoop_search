51089245	O Primeiro Amor	O Primeiro Amor is a Brazilian telenovela produced and broadcast by TV Globo. It premiered on 24 January 1972 and ended on 20 October 1972, with a total of 227 episodes. It is the tenth "novela das sete" to be aired at the timeslot.
